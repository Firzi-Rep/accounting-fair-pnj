Accounting Fair 2023

Accounting Fair 2023 is an Educative Competitive Event. The 2023 Accounting Fair consists of one pre-event content and 4 main event content, namely Entrepreneur In Action (EIA), Olimpiade Akuntansi dan Pasar Modal (OAPM), Accounting Smart Competition (ASC), and Bazaar.

THIS YEAR'S GRAND THEME

The Accounting Fair 2023 has chosen the theme of Generation Z with the concept of Nature. This decision is based on a natural phenomenon that has been discussed by the general public, particularly among the younger generation, commonly referred to as Gen Z. It is hoped that the young generation will continue to monitor and preserve nature for a brighter future, in line with the AF tagline for this year: "Building a better future with the young generation." The design concept for Accounting Fair 2023 consists of representations of rural areas, forests, urban areas, and mountainous regions as a symbol of nature
